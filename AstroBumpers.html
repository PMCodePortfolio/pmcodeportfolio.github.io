AstroBumpers

For my self-employed placement year, I designed, developed and published the mobile game AstroBumpers, which was space-themed and inspired by the arcade game Pong.

As the concept's unfamiliarity complicated the design process, a prototype was created and iterated in GameMaker. The game's development continued in Unity, programmed in C#, visual assets created in Gimp, InkScape and Blender, and audio produced in Audacity and GarageBand.

The mobile game was released on Google Play and App Store for Android and iPhone devices respectively.

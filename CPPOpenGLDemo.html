C++ & OpenGL Demonstration

To explore further into C++ and OpenGL, I created this individual project which utilised OpenGL’s Programmable Render Pipeline and features:

  • Vertex Colouring & Texturing
  • Overlaying & Interactive Graphics
  • Perspective & Orthographic Projections
  • Translation, Rotation, Scale Transformations
  • Transformations & Vertex Based Animations
  • Object Instancing
  • Lighting Effects
    • Directional
    • Point-Based
    • Colouring
    • Vertex Emissions
  • Window Scalability

The project also implements:

  • Vertex Shading - calculates vertex variables such as position, colour, lighting and normals
  • Fragment Shading - renders each pixel from vertex shader data and textures
